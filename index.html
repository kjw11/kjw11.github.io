I am a Ph.D. student at The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), supervised by Prof. Helen Meng. I am extensively interested in dicriminative modeling for speech and spoken language, including speech and speaker recognition, conversational diagnostics, and conversational human-computer interaction. Currently, my work focuses on Neurocognitive Disorder (NCD) detection using speech and language analytics, aiming to facilitate universal non-invasive screening.

Prior to attending CUHK, I worked at Centor for Speech and Language Technology (CSLT) @ Tsinghua University, undere the supervision of Prof. Dong Wang , concentrating on robust speaker recognition. I obtained my Bachelor's degree from Jilin University, where I participated in optical signal processing research as an undergraduate intern.

Feel free to contact me if you have any question about my works.
