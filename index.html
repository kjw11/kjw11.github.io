I am a Ph.D. student at The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), supervised by Prof. Helen Meng. I am interested in general speech-based recognition tasks, especially Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR), Neurocognitive Disorder (NCD) detection, and Speaker Recognition (SRE).

Before joining CUHK, I worked at Centor for Speech and Language Technology (CSLT) @ Tsinghua University, supervised by Prof. Dong Wang . I obtained my Bachelor's degree from Jilun University (JLU).
